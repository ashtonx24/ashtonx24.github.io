Hi — I’m Ashar Parvez Chougle. I build systems that work within limits, not against them. I graduated with a B.Sc. in Information Technology (University of Mumbai, May 2025) and shifted focus from “features” to architecture.

I created inkdna — a privacy-first fingerprinting API for PDFs and images that helps authors and publishers trace leaks without DRM, watermarks, or friction. It stamps per-buyer PDFs at checkout (via a lightweight JS plugin) and can detect a leak from a single screenshot.

I work with Python, API design, Cloudflare R2 & Workers, Supabase, and edge caching — not to chase scale, but to build clarity that lasts.

If you’re building something lean and real, I’m open to conversations and collaborations.
